ICSSEA 2017

Sponsored by AFIS (the French Association for Systems Engineering) and INCOSE, co-organized by CNAM (Conservatoire National des Arts et Métiers), and the Génie Logiciel quarterly, the 28th edition of the ICSSEA Conference (International Conference on Software & Systems Engineering and their Applications) will be held in Paris on May 31-June 2, 2017. By gathering actors from across the enterprise and research worlds, it aims at providing a critical survey of the status of tools, methods, and processes for elaborating software & systems.

In addition to regular sessions, the conference will host an associated event: NEPTUNE 2017, the 14th edition of the yearly workshop dedicated to Model-Based Systems / Software Engineering.
