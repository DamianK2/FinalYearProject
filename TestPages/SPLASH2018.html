Welcome to SPLASH 2018!

The ACM SIGPLAN conference on Systems, Programming, Languages and Applications: Software for Humanity (SPLASH) embraces all aspects of software construction and delivery to make it the premier conference at the intersection of programming, languages, and software engineering. SPLASH 2018 will take place in Boston, Massachusetts, USA from Sun 4 - Fri 9 November 2018.
