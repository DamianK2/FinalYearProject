ISPDC 2018

The 17th IEEE International Symposium on Parallel and Distributed Computing,
25-28 June 2018, Geneva, Switzerland
Venue

Venue

The conference will take place at the haute école du paysage, d’ingénierie et d’architecture (hepia), 4 Rue Prairie, 1202, Geneva – University of Applied Sciences and Arts, Western Switzerland (HES-SO).

See this plan for more details.

Travel

by Air: From the airport: take the bus, line 10 (station: Prairie ) or the train (Cornavin rail station)
by Train: hepia is 5 minutes walk from the railway station (Cornavin).
By Bus: You can take one of these buses from the “Cornavin” or “22 canton” stations:

  • 06, Direction: Vernier,
  • 10, Direction: Aéroport,
  • 19, Direction: Vernier Village
  • 27, Direction: Vernier Tours Lignon.

Get off at Prairie station.
